Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get set up and when can I go live ?

    Setting up is so easy! Refer to Vendor's login page to login/register your business. When you login to your dashboard you will be able to complete the process by setting up your venue information, which will appear to customers on the Bridge Order app. Ensure to upload your restaurant’s photos before you can go live.

    You will receive a notification on your dashboard on any reservation and ordering.

  • How do I set up my venue QR code?

    Upon completion of registration a unique QR code will be automatically generated for your venue.

  • What’s the cost of registering my business?

    Registration ass a vendor on the app is absolutely free.
